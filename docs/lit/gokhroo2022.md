# gokhroo2022

Paper 2 baseline. Read in full 2026-07-16: cold $^{87}$Rb in a MOT around the fibre (NOT a warm vapour, as an earlier draft of ours said), driving $5S_{1/2} (F{=}2) \to 6S_{1/2} (F''{=}2)$ at 993 nm -- our exact line -- with an M Squared SolsTis Ti:Sapph quoted at sub-MHz linewidth, and a counter-propagating 993 nm vapour cell as the Doppler-free frequency reference. The two-peak/dip profile is explained at hypothesis level ("We speculate that ... resonance scattering induced pushing ... becomes the dominant effect"); dip positions are compared with dressed-state resonances, but there is no fitted lineshape model, and Casimir-Polder / van der Waals / surface potentials appear nowhere in the paper. Published title says "light fields"; the arXiv title says "electric fields". DOI verified via Crossref 2026-07-16.
