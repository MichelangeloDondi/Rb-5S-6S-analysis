# pache2026

EIT-cooling / atom-source thread (companion to pennetta2026, same group + 450 nm fibre). Lambda-enhanced gray molasses = 6x loading over red-detuned PGC; ~4000 Cs atoms in a 24 uK trap, optical depth $>$140, collisional blockade over ~1 mm; EIT-assisted cooling raises storage to 400(9) ms (5x), works with two co-propagating guided fields at a few hundred pW. Relevant to guided-atom source optimisation (the strongest Lan-pitch match) and Paper 2's loading/cooling toolkit. Verified from the held arXiv PDF 2026-07-17.
